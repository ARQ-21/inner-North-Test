About Us

Inner North, founded by combat veteran Suhey Fisher, is a minority-owned small business dedicated to bringing holistic health and mental wellness to underserved communities. We empower corporate organizations to cultivate resilience, prevent burnout, and enhance well-being, with a focus on diversity and inclusion. Our approach blends mindfulness and resilience-building techniques, promoting a healthier, more supportive workplace culture.

Our expertise in high-stress environments, from military to healthcare, informs our empathetic and culturally aware methods. Inner North is committed to making workplaces thrive through holistic health, connecting individuals to their inner strength, and fostering safe, inclusive spaces for all.

Foundation

Inner North’s foundation is built on mindfulness and resilience techniques that address workplace stress and burnout. Our programs, tailored for high-stress environments, emphasize empathy, cultural awareness, and practical tools to support daily well-being and mental health.

Partnering with organizations, we create awareness around burnout, stress management, and resilience, helping build company cultures that value diversity, equity, and inclusion.

BIO

I’m Suhey Fisher, a combat veteran and founder of Inner North, bringing together my experiences from the military, VA work, and corporate healthcare. I understand the unique challenges veterans and those in high-stress roles face, and I advocate for mental wellness and inclusion.

At Inner North, we’re committed to empowering individuals to find resilience, connect with their inner strength, and build supportive workplaces. By prioritizing underrepresented communities, we aim to make holistic health accessible and align personal well-being with corporate goals.
