Mindfulness & Resilience Workshops for Veteran Employees

Our Mindfulness and Resilience Workshop is designed to support veteran employees in transitioning from military to corporate life, focusing on skills to manage PTSD, workplace stress, and resilience. Through trauma-informed tools and small group sessions, veterans connect and develop support networks with peers who understand their journey. This workshop empowers veterans and their families to confidently adapt to civilian careers, honoring their service by equipping them with essential tools for personal and professional success.
